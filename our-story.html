Our Story

Building the future of banking, one customer at a time

Founded in 2014, Atom Bank is the UK's first app-based bank. We're on a mission to redefine what a bank should be, making banking simpler, more transparent, and better value for everyone.

Our Journey

From a bold idea to a leading digital bank

2014

The Beginning

Atom Bank was founded with a vision to create a bank that works for customers, not against them. We received our banking license application and began building our revolutionary app-based banking platform.

2015

Banking License

We became the UK's first app-based bank to be granted a full regulatory banking license by the Bank of England. This was a major milestone in our journey to revolutionize banking.

2016

Launch Day

Atom Bank officially launched to the public, offering competitive fixed-term savings accounts through our innovative app. We also secured £135 million in funding from investors including BBVA.

2017

Expanding Our Offerings

We launched our digital mortgage products, bringing our innovative approach to the UK mortgage market. We also introduced business loans to support SMEs across the country.

2018

Growing Customer Base

We celebrated reaching 100,000 customers and £1.3 billion in deposits. Our commitment to offering competitive rates and excellent customer service continued to attract new customers.

2019

Award-Winning Banking

Atom Bank won multiple industry awards for our innovative approach to banking, including "Best Online Savings Provider" and "Best Fixed Rate Mortgage Provider".

2020

Supporting Customers Through COVID-19

During the global pandemic, we provided support to our customers through payment holidays and continued to offer competitive savings rates despite economic uncertainty.

2021

Four-Day Working Week

We became one of the first UK companies to introduce a four-day working week for all employees without reducing pay, demonstrating our commitment to work-life balance and employee wellbeing.

2022

Continued Growth

We reached £3 billion in mortgage lending and continued to expand our product offerings to meet the evolving needs of our customers.

2023

Looking to the Future

Today, we continue to innovate and grow, staying true to our mission of making banking better for everyone. With a focus on cutting-edge technology and customer-centric design, we're building the bank of the future.

Our Mission

At Atom Bank, we're on a mission to change banking for the better. We believe that banking should be simple, transparent, and designed around the customer's needs.

We're committed to:

  • Offering competitive rates that give real value to our customers
  • Providing a seamless digital experience that makes banking easy
  • Being transparent in everything we do, with no hidden fees or charges
  • Putting our customers at the heart of every decision we make
  • Using technology to solve real problems and improve people's financial lives
