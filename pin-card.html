Security Tips

  • Choose a Strong PIN

    Avoid using easily guessable PINs like birthdays, sequential numbers (1234), or repeated digits (1111). Use a unique PIN for each card.

  • Shield Your PIN

    Always cover the keypad when entering your PIN at ATMs or payment terminals. Be aware of your surroundings and watch for suspicious devices or people.

  • Regularly Monitor Your Accounts

    Check your account statements regularly for unauthorized transactions. Set up transaction alerts to be notified of any activity on your cards.

  • Freeze Your Card When Not in Use

    If you're not actively using your card, consider freezing it temporarily. This adds an extra layer of security against unauthorized use.
