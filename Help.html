Frequently Asked Questions

You can apply for a mortgage online through our website, by phone, or by visiting one of our branches. Our online application takes about 20 minutes to complete, and you'll need information about your income, employment, and the property you're interested in.

Typically, you'll need: business financial statements (profit/loss, balance sheet), business tax returns (2 years), personal tax returns of owners (2 years), business license, and a debt schedule. Requirements may vary based on loan type and amount.

Mortgage approvals typically take 2-3 weeks after submitting all required documents. Business loans can take 1-4 weeks depending on complexity. You'll receive regular updates throughout the process from your loan officer.
