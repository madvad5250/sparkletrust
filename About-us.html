Our Journey

1995

Founded in a Small Office

SparkleBank began as a local community bank with just 5 employees, serving the financial needs of our neighborhood.

2005

Expanded to 10 Branches

After a decade of success, we grew to serve the entire state with innovative banking solutions.

2015

Launched Digital Banking

We embraced technology to provide seamless online and mobile banking experiences for our customers.

2023

Serving Over 1 Million Customers

Today, we're proud to be a trusted financial partner for individuals and businesses across the country.

Community Involvement

At SparkleBank, we believe in giving back to the communities that have supported our growth. Each year, we:

  • Donate 1% of our profits to local charities
  • Sponsor financial literacy programs in schools
  • Support small business development initiatives
  • Provide volunteer hours through our employee program

In 2022 alone, we contributed over $2 million to community causes and our employees volunteered more than 5,000 hours.
