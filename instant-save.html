Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our Instant Save account

What is the minimum amount I can deposit?

You can open an Instant Save account with as little as $1. There is no maximum deposit limit, but the FSCS protection only covers up to $85,000 per person.

How quickly can I access my money?

You can withdraw your money at any time without notice or penalties. Transfers to your nominated account typically arrive within 1-2 hours, but may take up to one working day.

How is interest calculated and paid?

Interest is calculated daily on your balance and paid into your account monthly. The current rate is 4.15% AER/Gross (variable).

Can I have more than one Instant Save account?

Yes, you can open multiple Instant Save accounts to help you organize your savings for different goals.

Is my money protected?

Yes, eligible deposits are protected by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS) up to $85,000 per person.
