Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about first time buyer mortgages

How much deposit do I need as a first time buyer?

As a first time buyer with Atom Bank, you'll typically need a minimum deposit of 10% of the property's value. However, the larger your deposit, the better the interest rate you're likely to be offered. We also have some mortgage products that require a 15% deposit, which come with more competitive rates.

How much can I borrow for my first mortgage?

The amount you can borrow depends on several factors, including your income, outgoings, credit history, and the size of your deposit. As a general rule, you can typically borrow between 4-4.5 times your annual income. Use our mortgage calculator to get an estimate, or apply for a Decision in Principle for a more accurate figure.

What fees will I need to pay?

There are several fees associated with getting a mortgage, including:

  • Arrangement fee: This is charged by the lender for setting up the mortgage. With Atom Bank, this ranges from £0-£999 depending on the product.
  • Valuation fee: We offer free basic valuations on most of our mortgage products.
  • Legal fees: You'll need to pay a solicitor to handle the legal aspects of buying a property.
  • Stamp Duty: As a first time buyer, you may be eligible for Stamp Duty relief on properties up to £425,000.

How long does the mortgage application process take?

The time it takes to get a mortgage can vary, but with Atom Bank, you can typically get a Decision in Principle in minutes. The full application process, from application to offer, usually takes 2-3 weeks, depending on the complexity of your application and how quickly you can provide the required documentation.

What documents will I need to provide?

You'll typically need to provide:

  • Proof of identity (passport or driving license)
  • Proof of address (utility bills, bank statements)
  • Proof of income (payslips, P60, tax returns for self-employed)
  • Bank statements (usually the last 3 months)
  • Details of any existing debts or financial commitments
With Atom Bank's digital application process, you can upload these documents securely through our app or online portal.
