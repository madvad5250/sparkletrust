Our story

Sparkle Trust Consolidated Bank was founded in 2014 to make things better by offering customers better and fairer banking. We were awarded our banking licence in 2015, then officially launched in 2016. We've gone from strength to strength since, growing to a large team of talented people headquartered in sunny Durham. Our app-based savings accounts and mortgages have helped thousands of customers achieve their financial goals, which has led to us being ranked number 1 in Trustpilot's Best in Bank category.
